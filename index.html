Why Movies and Meals?

We've all been through this dilemna before: You want to watch a movie, but you need to eat something to really enjoy the experience. The idea of choosing a movie AND a meal are way too much for you to deal with right now. Life is hard, and after a long day of work you want to be able to turn your mind off. Well your prayers have now been answered with: Movies & Meals! Let us do the heavy lifting for you. All you have to do is select your mood and based on your selection we'll give you a movie and a meal to match the vibe. Don't like our suggestion? Just click the button to sift through our collection until you find the right one for you. Life just got a little more easier.
